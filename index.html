Me

I loved design before I ever had the words for it. Before I understood layouts, typography, or branding, I was already paying attention to why certain things looked so good to me. It could be a goofy T-shirt with corny type, a pop-art graphic, an NBA logo, high-end clothing, or an album cover that carried a whole feeling before you even heard the music. I was drawn to things that felt emotional, intentional, and visually strong. Back then, I would not have called it design, but I just knew I liked art and anything that could make people feel something. That interest stayed with me long before I ever seriously studied graphic design.
